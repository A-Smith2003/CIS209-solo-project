Our Story

Elephant Bookstore — Muscle Shoals, Alabama (est. 2012)

Founded in 2012, Elephant Bookstore is a beloved independent bookstore located in Muscle Shoals, Alabama, offering a welcoming space for readers, learners, and book lovers of all kinds.

Known for its carefully curated selection of bestselling novels, timeless classics, children’s stories, and local interest titles, Elephant Bookstore celebrates the joy of reading and lifelong discovery. With a cozy atmosphere and a strong connection to the community, it serves as more than just a shop—it’s a gathering place where stories are shared, ideas are sparked, and the love of literature continues to grow.

Librarian's Choice

Title: The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig

Nora Seed feels overwhelmed by regret and the belief that she has let everyone down, prompting her to attempt to end her life. She finds herself in a magical library managed by her childhood librarian, Mrs. Elm. Each book on the shelves allows her to experience a life she could have lived if she had made different choices—such as becoming a glaciologist, an Olympian, or staying in a different relationship. The book is a fast-paced,, philosophical exploration of what makes life worth living.

Title: The Thursday Murder Club

Author: Richard Osman

Set in the peaceful Cooper's Chase retirement village, the club meets weekly in the Jigsaw Room to discuss unsolved mysteries. However, the group—a former spy, a nurse, a psychiatrist, and a union activist—soon find a real murder on their doorstep. They use their combined skills and unassuming, elderly status to investigate, much to the dismay of local detectives. The story combines suspense with humor, charm, and poignant reflections on aging and friendship. The book is the first in a best-selling series by British television presenter Richard Osman.

Title: Frankenstein

Author: Mary Shelley

Victor Frankenstein, a young scientist from Geneva discovers the secret of imparting life to inanimate matter. He constructs a creature from body parts, but upon bringing it to life, he is horrified by his creation and abandons it. The intelligent but shunned creature, longing for companionship, becomes vengeful after being rejected by society, ultimately destroying Victor's life.
